About This Test

How the MBTI Coffee Match Test Works

The MBTI Coffee Match Test analyzes your personality through 8 cafe scenario questions. Each question evaluates one of the four MBTI dimensions: Extraversion/Introversion, Sensing/Intuition, Thinking/Feeling, and Judging/Perceiving. Based on your answers, you are matched with one of 16 coffee drinks that best reflects your personality traits and coffee preferences.

What Your Coffee Match Says About You

Your matched coffee reveals insights about your energy style, social preferences, and decision-making approach. Whether you resonate with Espresso's concentrated intensity, Caramel Macchiato's sweet energy, or Cold Brew's powerful calm, your coffee match helps you understand your personality in a fun, relatable way.
